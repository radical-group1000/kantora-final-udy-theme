- Preparation, convening and holding of general assemblies of the partners or shareholders and preparation of the related corporate documents, entering of the resolutions in the relevant registers;

- Legal advice and representation at all stages of insolvency proceedings, including at creditors’ meetings;

- Taking actions to secure the receivables and interests of the client in the cases where he has the capacity of creditor, claiming, initiating proceedings and legal representation in cases of cancellation of transactions and actions of a debtor, damaging the creditors;

- Participation in negotiations for settlement of the relations between the parties involved in the insolvency proceedings, consultations with regard to the preparation of a reorganization plan or the allocation of the collected assets;

- Full interaction with the appointed trustee.

- Legal advice and assistance in proceedings for issuance of different types of administrative acts;

- Appeal against administrative acts of the state, municipal and tax administration;

- Legal advice and preparation of documentation for participation in public procurement;

- Legal representation before all government departments and municipal authorities;

- Legal representation and legal protection during administrative actions before all courts.

- Legal advice concerning the application of current tax legislation;

- Legal representation and protection of workers/employees and employers during labour disputes;

- Legal representation during collective labour disputes;

- Protection of the clients’ interests during disputes related to violations of the labour legislation.

- Research and identification of all rights and encumbrances on a particular property, preparation of legal analysis;

- Participation in negotiations, preparation and conclusion of deals and legal assistance in their completion;

- Legal assistance before state and municipal authorities;

- Legal advice and assistance in the selection of suitable objects for investment in the country, as well as analysis of the most appropriate forms for acquisition of property rights or limited property rights by foreign investors, including through the incorporation of a commercial company;

- Research and legal advice related to admission of development procedure and Detailed Site Development Plan projects;

- Assistance in regulatory procedures and conduct method analysis;

- Preparation of opinions and implementation of a procedure for changing the designation of agricultural lands, forests and forest fund lands;

- Representation of clients related to various administrative procedures when obtaining all the necessary permits and licenses;

- Legal advice concerning complicated regulatory issues, preparation of commercial contracts, contracts of irrevocable right to use, agreements for the provision of different types of electronic communication services, as well as the relevant conditions of use;

- Providing legal advice concerning the protection of authors’ and contractors’ rights with regard to copyright and related rights, as well as documentation and proof of ownership of works / performances and the possession of copyrights;

- Legal counseling of copyright holders during and after the creation of works, as well as analysis and assessment of competing rights of third persons;

- Preparation, revision and analysis of copyright agreements, license agreements and other documents;

- Legal representation in disputes, sub-departmental to arbitration structures;
